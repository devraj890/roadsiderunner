About

The Fuel Delivery on Demand and On Road Vehicle Breakdown Assistance systems offer a comprehensive solution for the needs of vehicle owners while on the road. The automobile industry has seen rapid growth in recent years, leading to an increase in fuel consumption. Unfortunately, there may be situations where a vehicle runs out of fuel, making it difficult for the owner to push the vehicle to the nearest fuel station. In other cases, individuals may be in unfamiliar locations and unaware of nearby gas stations to refuel their vehicles. The Fuel Delivery on Demand system addresses these issues by providing a platform for users to book fuel at any location and time. The user-friendly application allows users to search for fuel stations by either locality or station name and make their bookings online.
On the other hand, the On Road Vehicle Breakdown system provides a solution for individuals who encounter mechanical issues while on the road. The system connects registered users with trusted mechanics, ensuring that only legally approved mechanics are enlisted. This helps to prevent individuals from falling prey to unscrupulous mechanics who may charge exorbitant fees for their services. The system also includes a feedback system, where users can provide updates about the services they received. This helps to maintain the quality of services provided by the mechanics enlisted in the system.
